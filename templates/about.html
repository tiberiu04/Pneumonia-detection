🔬 Research Foundation

Our pneumonia detection system is built on cutting-edge research in computer vision and medical imaging. The model architecture is inspired by successful implementations in medical image analysis, particularly those published in leading conferences like MICCAI and journals such as Medical Image Analysis.

Key Research Areas:
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for medical image classification
  • Transfer Learning techniques for limited medical datasets
  • Data Augmentation strategies for improving model robustness
  • Attention Mechanisms for focusing on relevant image regions
